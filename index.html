Auto Email (EmailJS) ● Not configured
Free 200 emails/month. Sign up at emailjs.com → create Gmail service → create templates → paste IDs below → Save Settings → Test.
Subject-based threading (Gmail-compatible):
• Quotation email subject: Quotation - Ref#{{job_number}} - {{company}}
• Invoice email subject: Approval - Ref#{{job_number}} - {{company}}
• QC/Ready email subject: Device Ready - Ref#{{job_number}} - {{company}}
Same subject = Gmail threads them together automatically. Add {{subject}} in your EmailJS template subject line.
Template variables — use in EmailJS template body:
{{to_name}} {{to_email}} {{job_number}} {{vendor_job}} {{b2b_ref}} {{company}} {{device}} {{problem}} {{subject}} {{shop_name}} {{shop_phone}} {{shop_email}}

Quotation / Invoice email extras:
{{grand_total}} {{subtotal}} {{gst_total}} {{gst_mode}} {{estimate}} {{final_amount}} {{paid_amount}} {{balance}} {{pay_status}} {{line_items}}

For full HTML quote table in email body:
{{quote_html}} — pastes the full formatted quotation/invoice table directly into the email
{{quote_table}} — plain-text version (for simple templates)

PDF Attachment: After clicking email button, a confirmation dialog lets you open the print window to save as PDF and attach manually — EmailJS free plan does not support file attachments.
📊 View sent/failed email history in Comms Log or the dedicated Email Log page (sidebar).
